# Business Overview: PrecisionPerformance.com.au

## 1. Mission and Mantra
- **Mission**: To eliminate ambiguity in equine performance through high-precision biological analysis of urine and saliva.
- **Mantra**: "Go by the Numbers, Trust the Numbers, No Guessing."

## 2. Core Purpose
Precision Performance is an **equine performance biochemistry platform** focused on racehorses. The platform provides trainers with mathematical certainty by capturing, analyzing, and reporting on physiological metrics.

## 3. Target Audience
- **Primary**: Elite Thoroughbred Trainers.
- **Secondary**: Performance-focused Horse Owners and Stable Staff.

## 4. Scalability Principles
The business is built on a **Modular Platform** model (Phased structure):
1. **Public Website**: Brand authority, service definitions, contact flows, and e-commerce shop.
2. **Member Platform**: Secure dashboard for private data, reports, and horse profile management.
3. **Data-Entry App**: A phone-first application for stable staff to submit records in real-time.

## 5. Strategic Recommendations
- **Mobile-First Data Entry**: Record submission must be optimized for stable environments.
- **Structured Fields**: Physiological data (pH, Brix, Salts) must be stored in standardized numeric fields, not free-text.
- **Empirical Guidance**: Interpretations and reports are driven by verifiable benchmarks (RBTI thresholds), not subjective observations.
