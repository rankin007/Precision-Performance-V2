# Project 018: Trainer Sign-up & Approval Workflow

## Objective
Establish a secure, high-barrier application process for trainers to join the PrecisionPerformance.com ecosystem.

## Requirements
- **Multi-step Application:** 
    - Step 1: Professional credentials and stable location.
    - Step 2: Agreement to the "No Guessing" data protocols.
    - Step 3: Account creation (Supabase Auth).
- **Admin Approval Queue:** A private dashboard for Precision Performance admins to review and approve/reject applications.
- **Automated Onboarding:** Triggering the "Welcome" email and "Quick Start Guide" delivery upon successful approval.
- **Data Security:** Ensure application data is encrypted and protected by RLS before the account is fully activated.
