# Project 003: Shop and Product Commerce Integration

## Objective
Develop a high-end e-commerce experience within the portal for the sale of equine analysis kits, supplements, and specialised equipment.

## Requirements
- **Product Range:** Urine/Saliva collection kits, RBTI-aligned supplements, and testing hardware.
- **Membership Logic:** Integrate with Supabase Auth to provide tiered pricing (e.g., Trainer vs. Client discounts).
- **Checkout Flow:** Minimised friction, adhering to the "Breathe" philosophy.
- **Inventory Tracking:** Real-time stock levels synced with the database.
- **Order History:** Accessible within both Trainer and Client portals for easy re-ordering.
