# Project 020: Horse Metrics History & Long-term Trends

## Objective
Develop a high-precision historical data view to identify correlations between bio-metrics and performance outcomes over time.

## Requirements
- **Time-Series Engine:** View data across 7-day, 30-day, 90-day, and 12-month intervals.
- **Multi-Metric Overlay:** Compare Sugar (Brix), Conductivity (C), and pH on a single synchronised timeline.
- **Deviation Analytics:** Automatically calculate the percentage of time spent within the "Healing Ranges."
- **Event Correlation:** Map "Race Days" and "Feeding Changes" directly onto the timeline to see immediate biological impacts.
